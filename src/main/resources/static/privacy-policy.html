Privacy Policy

At FarmConnect, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our marketplace.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identity: Name, Email, and Phone Number provided during registration.
  • Payment Data: We use Razorpay to process payments securely. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers.
  • Shipping Info: Address details required to deliver fresh produce to your doorstep.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly for order processing, account management, and providing updates on your purchases. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Razorpay Integration

Our checkout process is integrated with Razorpay, a secure payment gateway. All transactions are encrypted and processed by Razorpay's secure servers in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
